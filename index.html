Our Story

Rooted in Missouri Since 2015

Midwest Organics Inc has been a cornerstone of agricultural and manufacturing innovation since our founding in 2015. Based in Sikeston, Missouri, we've grown from humble beginnings to become a leader in organic solutions across multiple industries.

Our commitment to quality and sustainability drives everything we do. From pet products to horticulture solutions, we combine traditional farming knowledge with modern manufacturing techniques to deliver exceptional products.
